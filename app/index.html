About SMT

Smart Money Token (SMT) is a decentralized cryptocurrency token created in the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), and provides unique use cases for its holders.

This is a full-fledged deflationary token for passive income by stacking, creating liquidity pools, and paying rewards to holders for holding coins. It is based on rigorous mathematical calculation and in-depth analysis of the economic model.

Features of Smart Money Token

During the campaign (before we create the Smart Money Swap and NFT Market) — enjoy passive income in SMT, just by keeping Smart Money Token on your personal wallet.

The SMT smart contract also provides automatic burning of tokens, which will make your investment more valuable and the saved assets more expensive.

Automatic rewards to holders and token burning are available due to a laid down commission which will be charged for buying or selling SMT.

Save your Smart Money Tokens before we launch Smart Money Swap — stake them or create a liquidity pool to increase the number of tokens you hold and, as a result, your passive income.

HOW TO BUY

Step 1: Create a Wallet

On Google Chrome, visit metamask.io to download the extension and set up a wallet. Android and iOS users can download the Trust Wallet app on your phone.

Step 2: Send BNB to your BSC wallet

Buy BNB from Binance Exchange and transfer to your BSC wallet address.

Step 3: Swap BNB for SMT

Go to the DApps tab at the bottom, find PancakeSwap V2, click “Select a Currency”, enter the contract address in the search bar, and you should be able to find Smart Money Token.

Tip

If you are operating on the PC, please visit the official website of the PancakeSwap DEX (pancakeswap.finance/swap) and click “Connect Wallet” at the top right of the website.
